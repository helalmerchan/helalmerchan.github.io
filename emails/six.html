A Future to Believe In
 
We need your help to save the automotive performance aftermarket.

We share something in common. We love the thrill of automotive performance.
If we want that magic to continue, we need to take action.

Our industry is facing unprecedented regulatory challenges, and we will soon be at a crossroads that will determine our long-term fate. Fewer young adults are driving than ever before, and many view the automobile as a relic or an environmental disgrace.

It’s time to roll our sleeves up and address these issues. I respectfully ask for your support and the opportunity to serve the industry.

 
 
My Commitment To You!
 
Engage The Next Generation
Our industry is at tremendous risk if we do not engage with today’s young adults and kids.

Serve All SEMA Members Fairly without Politics
Filled with diverse businesses, from small companies to OEs, all members deserve an equal voice.

 
Assist Members with Adopting New Technology
Technology is the oxygen of the future. To stay competitive, we must adopt new technology.

Address Government Regulation
We must work with lawmakers to promote “reasonable” legislation that preserves our rights.

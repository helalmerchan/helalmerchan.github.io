How Do We Provide Quotes?

At The Used Racking Company we think “it’s good to talk” rather than just allow an online purchase so every call will be answered by a member of our team to talk through your requirements. You then have the benefit to talk directly to one of our experienced team who can provide helpful and impartial advice on all our products. In most cases, we can provide you with either an instant quotation over the telephone or we can provide a full written quotation providing a detailed specification, price and commercial terms of the sale.

For Larger Projects

For larger projects, we appreciate an initial telephone conversation might not be enough. Let us take the stress and hassle out of this from the word go by visiting your site to discuss your requirements in more detail. During the site visit we can measure up and recommend a solution that meets your needs and maximises your space completely. We can provide a fast and competitive priced quotation following on from our site visit. For all projects, we can offer a competitive and prompt full Installation service leaving you free to run your business.

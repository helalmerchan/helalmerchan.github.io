About

Savauno Massage is a theraputic massage clinic established to meet the growing medical massage needs of Camden County and surrounding areas. We offer therapeutic medical massage in partnership with physicians, chiropractors, physical therapists, occupational rehabilitation facilities, hospices, and the public.

We offer six comfortable treatment rooms equipped with professional massage tables of the highest quality to provide our clients with maximum comfort. All of our staff is highly trained and meets or exceeds the State of Georgia requirements.

Here at Savauno we pride ourselves on being the best in providing specialized treatment to meet the specific needs of our clients.

Our facility also incudes 1 hydrotherapy treatment room and a large conference room for training that seats up to 25 people comfortably.
